Aquorim — The Water Planet

Aquariom is a planet covered in endless oceans, floating arenas, underwater tunnels, and massive storms. Deep blue waters, neon reefs, glowing sea creatures, and floating cities shape the planet’s landscape. It rains 364 out of 365 days a year, making storms a natural part of life.

Home to the water signs — is known for its unpredictable tides and dangerous tournament environments. Competitors must survive giant waves, flooded arenas, and shifting underwater paths while competing for victory.

Despite its harsh conditions, Aquariom is considered one of the galaxy’s most mesmerizing planets, glowing with electric blue storms and bioluminescent oceans at night.
